Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients:

200g spaghetti
100g bacon
2 large eggs
50g grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook 200g spaghetti until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In a separate pan, cook 100g of bacon until crispy. Remove from heat, let it cool, and then chop it into small pieces.
  3. Step 3: In a mixing bowl, whisk together 2 large eggs, 50g of grated Parmesan cheese, and a pinch of pepper until well combined.
  4. Step 4: Add the cooked spaghetti and chopped bacon to the egg mixture. Toss until the pasta is well coated.
  5. Step 5: Season with salt and more pepper to taste.
  6. Step 6: Transfer the Pasta Carbonara to serving plates, garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pasta Carbonara!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

1 cup butter
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, cream together 1 cup of softened butter, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, and 3/4 cup brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Step 3: Beat in 2 large eggs one at a time, then stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  4. Step 4: In a separate bowl, combine 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, and 1 cup chocolate chips.
  5. Step 5: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Step 6: Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Step 8: Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Step 9: Enjoy your homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

2 cups mixed vegetables
2 tablespoons soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Heat 1 tablespoon of sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2: Add cubed tofu to the pan and cook until it becomes golden brown on all sides. Remove tofu from the pan and set aside.
  3. Step 3: In the same pan, add 2 cups of mixed vegetables and stir-fry until they become tender-crisp.
  4. Step 4: Return the cooked tofu to the pan with the vegetables.
  5. Step 5: Drizzle 2 tablespoons of soy sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Serve your delicious Vegetable Stir-Fry hot and enjoy!

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

500g chicken
1/2 cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a large skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter and sauté the chicken pieces until they are no longer pink. Remove chicken and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and the pureed tomatoes. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes are cooked down and the mixture thickens.
  3. Step 3: Stir in the garam masala, paprika, and turmeric. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Serve your creamy Butter Chicken with rice or naan bread and enjoy!
